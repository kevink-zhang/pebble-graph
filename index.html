Pebbles

Click to create a node. Press e on it to add 1 to its initial value. Click on two nodes to link them. Double click to unselect a node.

Press space to play/pause the simulation. Press r to clear the graph. Press s on a node to toggle sink. Press z to yeet the selected node. Select and shift click to yeet the edges between the two nodes.

The simulation will pause once it determines the graph is infinitely unstable. Unstable will then become true;

Graph Generation Inputs

Press t to generate a tree. Press k to generate a complete graph. Press c to generate a cycle graph.
