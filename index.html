Pebbles

Click to create a node. Double click on it to add 1 to its initial value. Click on two nodes to link them. Press e to unselect a node.

Press space to play/pause the simulation. Press s on a node to toggle sink. Press z to yeet the selected node.

The simulation will pause once it determines the graph is infinitely unstable. Unstable will then become true;

Graph Generation Inputs

Press t to generate a tree. Press k to generate a complete graph. Press c to generate a cycle graph.
